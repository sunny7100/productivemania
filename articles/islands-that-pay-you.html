7 Breathtaking Islands That Will Pay You to Move

8 min read March 2024

Imagine waking up to crystal-clear waters, pristine beaches, and the sound of waves gently lapping at your doorstep – all while getting paid to live there. These incredible islands around the world are offering financial incentives to attract new residents.

1. Antikythera, Greece

This small Greek island offers €500 per month for the first three years to new families willing to relocate. With a population of just 20 people, Antikythera provides a unique opportunity to experience authentic Greek island life while contributing to the community's revival.

2. Ponga, Spain

Located in the Asturias region, Ponga offers €3,000 per child to families who move there. This picturesque mountain village provides an escape from city life while offering financial support to help establish your new rural lifestyle.

3. Candela, Italy

This charming Italian town offers up to €2,000 to new residents who commit to living there for at least two years. With its rich history, beautiful architecture, and warm community, Candela represents the perfect blend of tradition and opportunity.

4. Albinen, Switzerland

Swiss village Albinen offers CHF 25,000 per adult and CHF 10,000 per child to new residents under 45 who commit to living there for 10 years. Nestled in the Alps, this village offers breathtaking mountain views and a high quality of life.

5. Mauritius

While not offering direct cash payments, Mauritius provides significant tax incentives and residency benefits for remote workers and retirees. The island nation's tropical paradise setting makes it an attractive destination for digital nomads.

6. Rubia, Spain

This Galician village offers free housing and €150 per month to new residents. With its lush green landscapes and proximity to the Atlantic coast, Rubia provides a peaceful retreat from modern urban stress.

7. Sambuca, Sicily

Famous for selling houses for €1, Sambuca also offers various incentives for renovation and settlement. This historic Sicilian town combines Mediterranean charm with incredible value for those seeking a new life in Italy.
